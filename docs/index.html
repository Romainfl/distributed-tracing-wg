Distributed Tracing Working Group

Our Mission

We define standards for interoperability between tracing tools.

The Chair of the Working Group is Alois Reitbauer. The W3C Team Contact for the Distributed Tracing Working Group is Philippe Le Hégaret.

Alois wrote an explainer of the work.

October 25-26 at TPAC 2018

We'll be meeting at TPAC 2018.

What is the Distributed Tracing?

  • A way to propagate tracing information across tiers instrumented with different tracing tools
  • A format to share trace data — full traces or fragments of traces — across tools for further interpretation
